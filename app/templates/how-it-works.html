How It Works

At DirectFarm, we make it easy for farmers to connect directly with customers, ensuring fresh, sustainable, and fairly-priced product. Here’s how our platform works:

For Farmers
1

Step 1: Register as a Farmer

Sign up on our platform and create your farmer profile by providing details about your farm, your location, and the types of product you offer.

2

Step 2: List Your Product

Upload details of your fresh product, including images, pricing, availability, and farming practices. Keep your inventory updated to let customers know what’s available.

3

Step 3: Receive Orders

Get notified when customers place orders. Manage orders easily through your farmer dashboard.

4

Step 4: Deliver or Arrange Pickup

Coordinate delivery or pickup based on the customers preferences. You can use your own logistics or arrange a convenient pickup spot.

5

Step 5: Get Paid

Receive secure payments directly into your account, ensuring fair and timely compensation for your hard work.

For Customers
1

Step 1: Sign Up as a Customer

Create a free account to explore the freshest, locally-sourced Product available near you.

2

Step 2: Browse Products

Search for fruits, vegetables, dairy, and other farm-fresh items using filters like location, price, and availability.

3

Step 3: Place Your Order

Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout. Choose your preferred delivery or pickup option.

4

Step 4: Enjoy Fresh Product

Get your product delivered to your doorstep or pick it up directly from the farm or a designated location.

5

Step 5: Provide Feedback

Rate your experience and share feedback to help farmers grow their business and maintain quality.
